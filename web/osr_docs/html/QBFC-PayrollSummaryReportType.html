PayrollSummaryReportType
The type of payroll report:
  • psrtEmployeeEarningsSummary
    This report shows information similar to the payroll summary report, but in a different layout. The report has a row for each employee and a column for each payroll item.
  • psrtPayrollLiabilityBalances
    This report lists the payroll liabilities the QuickBooks company owes to various agencies, such as the federal government, your state government, insurance plan administrators, labor unions, etc. The report covers unpaid liabilities incurred during the period of time shown in the From and To fields. If the company paid a liability incurred within the date range of the report, the report omits that liability, even if the payment occurred after the ending date of the report.
  • psrtPayrollSummary
    This report shows the total wages, taxes withheld, deductions from net pay, additions to net pay, and employer-paid taxes and contributions for each employee on the payroll.
