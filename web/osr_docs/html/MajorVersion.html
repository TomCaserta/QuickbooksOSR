MajorVersion
Indicates which version of QuickBooks is acting as the server:

For US editions of QuickBooks:
10 = QuickBooks 2002
11 = QuickBooks Enterprise version 1 (2002)
12 = QuickBooks 2003 or QuickBooks Enterprise version 2 or 3
13 = QuickBooks 2004 or QuickBooks Enterprise version 4
15 = US QuickBooks 2005 or QuickBooks Enterprise 5
16 = US QuickBooks 2006 or QuickBooks Enterprise 6
17 = US QuickBooks 2007 or QuickBooks Enterprise 7

For Canadian editions of QuickBooks:
12 = QuickBooks 2003
13 = QuickBooks 2004
14 = QuickBooks 2005
15 = QuickBooks 2006
16 = QuickBooks 2007

For UK editions of QuickBooks:
12 = QuickBooks 2003
13 = QuickBooks 2004
14 = QuickBooks 2005
15 = QuickBooks 2006
