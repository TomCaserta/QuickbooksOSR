Estimate
A QuickBooks estimate is a description of a sale that the QuickBooks user proposes to make to a current or prospective customer. An estimate might also be called a "bid" or a "proposal." In QuickBooks, estimates and invoices use similar fields, and an estimate can be converted into an invoice after the customer accepts the estimate.

An estimate is a non-posting transaction, so it does not affect a company’s balance sheet or income statement.
