MenuExtensionSubscription
Adds a menu extension to QuickBooks. (Each application can add only one menu extension to QuickBooks.)

  • Include a Submenu aggregate if you are adding several menu items that will be nested to the right of your AppName in the QuickBooks menu.
  • Include a MenuItem aggregate if you do not have submenu items. In this case, the MenuText will appear after your AppName (separated by a colon) in the QuickBooks menu.
