ListEventSubscription
A subscription to a QuickBooks list event. ListEventType and ListEventOperation can be repeated to subscribe to different combinations of list event type and operation. For example, to subscribe to Customer add and modify, Account add and modify, and Account delete, you could use the following two qbXML ListEventSubscription aggregates:

<ListEventSubscription>
<ListEventType>Customer</ListEventType>
<ListEventType>Account</ListEventType>
<ListEventOperation>Add</ListEventOperation>
<ListEventOperation>Modify</ListEventOperation>
</ListEventSubscription>

<ListEventSubscription>
<ListEventType>Account</ListEventType>
<ListEventOperation>Delete</ListEventOperation>
</ListEventSubscription>
