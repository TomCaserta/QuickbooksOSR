ReportBasis

If ReportBasis is
  • rbCash, the report bases income and expenses on the dates when money changed hands.
  • rbAccrual, the report bases income on the dates when customers were invoiced and bases expenses on the dates when bills were entered.
  • rbNone, the report uses the default ReportBasis, which is either the QuickBooks Preference setting or the QuickBooks default for a given type of report. In a report response, the SDK returns rbNone for reports that do not support ReportBasis. (The 1099 report, for example, has its own basis for generation.)
