VendorCreditAdd
Adds a vendor credit. A vendor credit is also known as a "bill credit". That is, it is a credit that a vendor owes you because you overpaid your bill, returned merchandise, or for some other reason. The VendorCredit is referenced in a BilPayment transaction, inside the SetCredit aggregate.

A VendorCredit should not be confused with a Credit Memo, which is something you owe your customer and which is referenced in a ReceivePayment request.
