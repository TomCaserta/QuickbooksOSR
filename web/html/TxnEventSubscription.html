TxnEventSubscription
A subscription to a QuickBooks transaction event. TxnEventType and TxnEventOperation can be repeated to subscribe to different combinations of transaction event type and operation. For example, to subscribe to Invoice add and modify, Bill add and modify, and Bill delete, you could use the following two qbXML TxnEventSubscription aggregates:

<TxnEventSubscription>
<TxnEventType>Invoice</TxnEventType>
<TxnEventType>Bill</TxnEventType>
<TxnEventOperation>Add</TxnEventOperation>
<TxnEventOperation>Modify</TxnEventOperation>
</TxnEventSubscription>

<TxnEventSubscription>
<TxnEventType>Bill</TxnEventType>
<TxnEventOperation>Delete</TxnEventOperation>
</TxnEventSubscription>
